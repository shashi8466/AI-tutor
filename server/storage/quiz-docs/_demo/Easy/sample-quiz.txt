Q1. What is the value of x in the equation 2x + 5 = 15?

A) 5
B) 10
C) 7.5
D) 20

Answer: A

Explanation: Subtract 5 from both sides: 2x = 10. Then divide by 2: x = 5.

Q2. Which of the following is a prime number?

A) 15
B) 23
C) 27
D) 33

Answer: B

Explanation: 23 is only divisible by 1 and itself, making it a prime number.

Q3. What is the area of a rectangle with length 8 cm and width 5 cm?

A) 13 cm²
B) 26 cm²
C) 40 cm²
D) 3 cm²

Answer: C

Explanation: Area of rectangle = length × width = 8 × 5 = 40 cm².
