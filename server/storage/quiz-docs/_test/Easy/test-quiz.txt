Q.1)Geometry & Trigonometry, Area and volume
The area of a rectangle is 2400 square centimeters. The width is 80 centimeters. What is the length?
A)30
Answer: 30
Explanation: Area = length × width. 2400 = 80 × l. l = 30.

Q.2)Algebra, Linear functions  
The product of p and 5 is q less than 8. Which equation represents this?
A)5p-q=8
B)5p=8-q
C)p/5-q=8
D)p/5=8-q
Answer: B
Explanation: 5p = 8-q
