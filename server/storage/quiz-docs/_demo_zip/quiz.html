Q.1)Geometry & Trigonometry, Area and volume

The area of a rectangle is 2400 square centimeters. The width of the rectangle is 80 centimeters. What is the length, in centimeters, of this rectangle?

A)30

Answer: 30

Explanation:

The area A in square centimeters, of a rectangle is the product of its length l and width w. Therefore, A=lw. Substituting 2400 for A and 80 for w yields 2400=80l. Dividing both sides by 80 yields l=30.

Q.3)Algebra, Linear functions

The product of p and 5 is q less than 8. Which equation represents the relationship between p and q?

A)5p-q=8

B)5p=8-q

C)p/5-q=8

D)p/5=8-q

Answer: B

Explanation:

The product of p and 5 is written as 5p. q less than 8 is 8-q. Equating the two gives 5p=8-q.
